— Notes & behaviour

Same shell.
One column. One thumb.

The mobile build keeps the desktop’s rhythm — eyebrow, large serif title, italic sub — but lets every grid collapse to a single column. The configurator is the only screen with horizontal motion: a swipeable floor rail so the plan can sit at the full width.

  • Nav · sticky, blurred, hamburger left and a “Reserve” CTA always visible right.
  • Hero · cropped portrait of the front elevation; the spec stripe becomes a horizontally-scrolling rail.
  • Specs & principles · multi-column desktop grids collapse to one or two on mobile.
  • Configurator · Unit A/B as a 2-segment, floors as a swipeable pill rail, plan SVG at the full screen width.
  • Plots table · becomes two stacked cards with the large unit numeral on the left.
  • Form · stacked fields, full-width submit, native iOS selects.
