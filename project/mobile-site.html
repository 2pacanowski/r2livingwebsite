— 01 · The development

A duplex you
actually design.

Porzeczkowa is two homes — Unit A and Unit B — built to the same exacting shell and then opened up to their owners. Walls move. Sockets move. Wet walls move.

The setting. Dziekanów Polski is the quietest corner of Łomianki — a sleepy north-west suburb of Warsaw, bordered by Kampinos National Park and the Vistula river.

The home. Two storeys, flat-roof modernism, cantilevered eaves, a covered carport per unit. South-facing back gardens, full-height living-room glazing, three bedrooms and a flex room above.

The promise. From plan stage through month four of build, you may move any interior wall, every socket, and the position of every wet wall.
