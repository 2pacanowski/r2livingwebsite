— 01 · The development

Two homes,
two distinct layouts.

Porzeczkowa 5 is two homes — Unit A and Unit B — sharing one plot and one architectural language, yet each drawn to its own floor plan. Not mirrored, not repeated: two individual houses behind a single, coherent façade.

The setting. Kiełpin is the quietest corner of Łomianki — a north-west suburb of Warsaw, bordered by Kampinos National Park on one side and the Vistula river on the other. A four-minute walk takes you to the bus stop on Kolejowa; the city centre is thirty minutes by car, or forty by bus.

The home. Two storeys, flat-roof modernism, cantilevered eaves and a covered carport per unit. South-facing back gardens, full-height living-room glazing, three bedrooms and a large utility & storage room above. Materials are deliberately ordinary — white through-coloured render, anthracite aluminium, oak floors inside — so the building ages gracefully and runs cheaply.

The two units. Unit A and Unit B are not mirror images of one another. Each has its own floor plan, its own room proportions and its own relationship to the garden and the street — two genuinely different houses that happen to share a wall and a roofline.

— 06 · Location

Between the forest
and the city.

Porzeczkowa sits in Kiełpin, on the quiet north-western edge of Łomianki — wedged between the Kampinos forest and the Vistula, yet a short hop from Warsaw.

Porzeczkowa 5 · Kiełpin, Łomianki 52.34° N · 20.88° E Open map →
— 01 · Warsaw, within reach

Twelve kilometres to the centre.

Buses from the Kolejowa stop reach the Młociny metro terminus in around twenty-five minutes, then a single line runs straight into central Warsaw. By car, the S7 expressway and Wisłostrada keep the city within a half-hour.

— 02 · Forest & river

A national park for a back garden.

Kampinos National Park begins just streets away — 385 km² of pine forest, inland dunes and wetland, a UNESCO biosphere reserve and one of the largest national parks in Europe. The Vistula runs along the town's other edge, with riverside paths and beaches.

— 03 · Łomianki, fully served

A town, not a dormitory.

Łomianki stands on its own: kindergartens and schools, the ICDS sport and culture centre, health clinics, supermarkets and a weekend market — daily life handled without driving into the city.
