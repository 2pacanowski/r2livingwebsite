— About R2 Living

A small studio that finishes what it starts.

R2 Living is a boutique developer of modern duplex homes in suburban Warsaw. We take on two to four projects a year — never more — so that each one gets the attention a home deserves.

We buy quiet plots on the green edge of the city, draw flat-roof houses in an honest, durable language, and build them in around fourteen months. Materials are deliberately ordinary — through-coloured render, anthracite aluminium, oak floors — so the buildings age gracefully and run cheaply.

The architect signs every plan and meets every buyer. No sales floor, no scripted pitch — just the people who design and build the homes.
