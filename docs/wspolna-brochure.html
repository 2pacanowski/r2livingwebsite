— 01 · The Development 2

Wspólna 20
Łomianki Dolne

Two expansive homes on a quiet residential street in Łomianki, nestled between Kampinos National Park and the Vistula River. Three storeys of flat-roof modernism — bold facade, generous glazing, north-east facing gardens.

Units 1 and 2 are mirror images of one another, built from a single plan. From the street they share an entrance path and parking — beyond that, everything turns private. Opposite orientations, separate gardens, each home entirely its own.

White exterior façade, anthracite windows, doors, balcony rails and roof flashing. Two timeless colours that don't fade, don't date, and don't need repainting to stay looking right.
