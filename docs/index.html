— About R2 Living

Built boutique.
Every project, fully.

R2 Living is the residential development brand of R2 Capital — a Warsaw-based real estate investment company.

We build one project at a time. Not because we have to — because we want every build to get the full weight of our attention. One project means one investor to call, one set of decisions to track, one site that matters completely.

We build in the quieter suburbs of Warsaw. Generous plots, good schools nearby, access to the city without the noise of it. The kind of neighbourhood a family chooses, not settles for.

— Boutique scale

One project at a time. Small enough that nothing slips, deliberate enough that nothing is rushed.

— Open by default

Progress updates, site visits, direct access to the investor. You're not waiting for news — you're part of the build.

— Honest specs

Materials, tolerances, and floor plans published from day one. No fine print, no surprises at handover.
